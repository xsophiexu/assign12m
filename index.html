About


I am a multimedia designer focusing on design thinking and social impact. As a first-year at NYU studying emerging media and human-centered design, I'm looking for opportunities in UX Design/Research and open to experiences involving interactive technology. I am familiar with tools such as Adobe Illustrator, Figma, Python and the design thinking process, working on projects and organizing events around tech and arts education. My high school background is in research, studio art, mathematics and coding.
